Our journey

From a single-brand distributor to a multi-division supply partner.

1994
Silicom Electronics is incepted in New Delhi
A small distribution office in Kirti Nagar, set up to bring quality test instruments and components to local manufacturers.
2000s
Authorized distribution for global T&M brands
Tektronix, then Microtest and others — Silicom becomes the go-to partner for oscilloscopes, hipot & transformer testers in North India.
2010s
Components division spins up
Adding semiconductors, MOSFETs, diodes, TVS, MLCCs and protection devices — a second division supporting EMS and automotive electronics customers.
2017
ISO 9001:2015 certified · GeM assessed
Process compliance for institutional and PSU customers. Active on the Government e-Marketplace platform since its inception.
2020s
EV, GaN & SiC focus
Expanding the components portfolio with wide-bandgap power, EV-specific fuses and chargers — supporting India's electrification push.
Today
Authorized brand partners · pan-India coverage
Two specialised divisions, 20+ employees, and a technical sales force covering the North India electronics belt and key clusters in the South.
